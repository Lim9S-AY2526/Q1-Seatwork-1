My Top 3 Favorite Movies/Series

Hi! This is an overview of my top 3 favorite movies/series. I'm Elise Lim, and I'm happy to share some of the films I love.

- My favorites are To All The Boys I've Loved Before, True Beauty, and Mean Girls.

- I'll give you a quick look at each movie and the main characters that make them great.

1. To All The Boys I've Loved Before (2018)


- Lara Jean, a high school girl, writes letters to the boys she is infatuated with but keeps them in her closet instead of mailing them. Things change when her sister discovers the letters.

Lara Jean | Peter Kavinsky | Kitty Covey | Gen

2. True Beauty (2020)


- True Beauty is a popular Korean drama following the story of a high school student named Lim Joo-kyung who was bullied for her looks and uses makeup to transform herself into a stunning "goddess" at her new school.

Lim Joo-Kyung | Lee Su-Ho | Han Seo-Jun

3. Mean Girls (2004)


- The film follows Cady Heron, a naive teenager who transfers to an American high school after years of homeschooling in Africa. Cady quickly befriends outcasts Janis and Damian with the trio forming a plan to exact revenge on Regina George, the leader of "the Plastics".


Karen Smith
